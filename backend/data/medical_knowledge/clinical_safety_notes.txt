Title: Clinical Safety Notes

Keywords:
safety, human review, conflict, uncertainty, clinical decision support

Evidence:
AI-generated medical outputs should be treated as clinical decision-support, not final diagnosis. Cases with moderate or high risk, conflicting agent outputs, or low-confidence findings should be reviewed by a qualified neurologist.

Clinical Use:
This evidence supports safety warnings, critic-agent review, conflict handling, and human-in-the-loop decision making.

Safety:
The system must not provide final diagnosis, prescription, or treatment decisions.
