Title: Parkinson Motor Symptoms

Keywords:
tremor, rigidity, bradykinesia, updrs, motor symptoms, medication response

Evidence:
Parkinsonian motor symptoms commonly include tremor, rigidity, bradykinesia, and postural instability. Elevated clinical scores such as UPDRS, along with tremor and rigidity findings, can support the need for neurologist review.

Clinical Use:
This evidence can support interpretation when the clinical agent detects moderate or high motor-related risk.

Safety:
This evidence is for clinical decision-support only and must be reviewed by a qualified clinician.
